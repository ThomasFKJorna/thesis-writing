* Holonomy

A kind of generalization of the notion of [[id:57213ae1-eff2-4442-b04b-84706076061f][Phase (quantum)]]

In general, a holonomy is the "failure of [[id:71b7fa31-7712-4a3a-a3ff-825298ed86cd][Parallel transport]] around closed cycles to preserve the geometrical information being transported" cite:Cohen2019.

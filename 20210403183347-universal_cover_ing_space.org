#+title: Universal cover(ing space)
#+filetags: definition topology

A [[file:20210403183001-covering_space.org][Covering space]] is called a *universal covering space* if it is [[file:20210403183452-simply_connected.org][Simply connected]].

It's called that way because the universal cover covers any connected cover.

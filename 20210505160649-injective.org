* Injective

A [[id:1c7c5c98-9acf-44e1-8921-4bb34eed0f92][function/mapping]] $f: A\rightarrow B$ is said to *injective* or *one to one* all points in $A$ map onto different points in $B$, or more formally

\[
\forall a,a' \in A \quad (f(a)=f(a'))\rightarrow(a=a')
\]

* Relation

** Munkres

A *relation* on a set $A$ is a subset $C$ of the [[id:905665b3-dcf5-4c1e-99e6-86831cc228a0][Cartesian Product]] $A \times A$.

So if $A$ is one dimensional, $C$ looks like $(a_1, a_2)$

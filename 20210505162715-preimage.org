* Preimage

The *preimage* of a function $f: A \rightarrow B$ is the set of all elements of A whose images lie in a subset $B_0$ of $B$ , $f^{-1}(B_0)$.

Formally
\[
f^{-1}(B_0)=\{a|f(a)\in B_0\}
\]

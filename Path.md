# Path

# Munkres

Given two points $x, y$ in the space $X$, a **path** in $X$ from $x$ to $y$ is a [[Continuity (function)|Continuous function]] $f: [a, b] \rightarrow X$ of some closed interval in $\mathbb{R}$ to $X$  such that $f(a)=x$ and $f(b)=y$

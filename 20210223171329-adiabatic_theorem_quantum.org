#+title: adiabatic theorem (quantum)
#+filetags: theorem stub

If a system is in a certain non-[[id:cab6efcd-b205-45e7-8873-31026d149490][degeneracy]] energy [[id:16c28b52-8850-460a-87b2-1d1cdb1f6580][eigenstate]] and its "parameters" are varied "sufficiently slowly", then the system will cling to that energy eigenstate.

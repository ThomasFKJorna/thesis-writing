* Berry Curvature

The *Berry Curvature* is the [[id:90b942e4-ed45-4fd2-909f-e74312c2dab0][Curl]] of the [[id:3dad96b9-a6bf-449f-981e-4e141f865dd5][Berry Connection]]
\begin{equation}
\mathbf{\Omega(k)}=\nabla_k\times\mathcal{A}(k)
    \label{eq:berrycurv}
\end{equation}

In a way it represent the amount of "curvature" (which in a way represents the "rotation") of parameter space.

* Proper time

*Proper time* $\Delta\tau=\Delta t-\Delta x-\Delta y -\Delta z$[fn:1] is the invariant quantity in [[file:special_relativity.org][Special Relativity]] and the way distance is measured in [[file:minkowski_spacetime.org][Minkowski Spacetime]].

Using the [[file:einstein_summation_notation.org][Einstein summation notation]], we can cleverly write this as
\begin{equation}
(d\tau)^2=dx_\mu \eta^{\mu\nu} dx_\nu
    \label{eq:propertime}
\end{equation}
where $\eta$ is the [[file:minkowski_metric.org][Minkowski Metric]] and $x_\mu$ and $x_\nu$ are [[file:four_vector.org][Four-vectors.]]

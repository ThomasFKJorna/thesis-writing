* Image

Let  $f: A \rightarrow B$ be a [[id:1c7c5c98-9acf-44e1-8921-4bb34eed0f92][function/mapping]]. Given a subset $A_0 \subseteq A$, the *image* of $A_0$ is the set of all values $f(a)$ with $a\in A_0$.

Formally
\[
f(A_0)=\{b|\exists a\in A_0: b=f(a)\}
\]


The inverse of the image is called the [[id:9bf1101b-fbba-4740-b101-7c98e9b0df41][Preimage]]

#+title: Gauge symmetry
#+filetags: definition physics symmetry

Any [[id:aa5fdff9-863c-4288-88ac-217f69b70cc1][internal]] and [[id:deba623a-e0d9-4db3-96e3-3cf22681ef43][Local symmetry]] is called a *gauge symmetry*, although sometimes physisists also use it to refer to any local symmetry, or one referring to a global or local change of scale or quantum mechanical phase.

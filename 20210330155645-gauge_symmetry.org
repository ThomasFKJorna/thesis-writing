#+title: Gauge symmetry
#+roam_tags: definition physics symmetry

Any [[file:20210330155810-internal_symmetry.org][internal]] and [[file:20210330160020-local_symmetry.org][Local symmetry]] is called a *gauge symmetry*, although sometimes physisists also use it to refer to any local symmetry, or one referring to a global or local change of scale or quantum mechanical phase.

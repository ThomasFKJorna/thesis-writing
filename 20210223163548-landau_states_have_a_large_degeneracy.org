* Landau states have a large degeneracy

Landau states have a large [[id:cab6efcd-b205-45e7-8873-31026d149490][degeneracy]] i.e. lots of states with different quantum numbers share the same energy [[id:16c28b52-8850-460a-87b2-1d1cdb1f6580][eigenstate]].

We can quantify this as

\begin{equation}
\mathcal{N} = \frac{AB}{\phi_0} \text{ where } A=\text{ area   and } \phi_0=\frac{2\pi\hbar}{e}
\label{eq:degen_landau}
\end{equation}

$\phi_0$ is the [[id:1780f5a9-7330-4491-be34-8f8f6e0e010c][quantum of flux]]

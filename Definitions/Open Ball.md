Given a [[Metric Space]] $r(X,d)$, an **open ball** $B_\epsilon(x)$ is the set of all points for which are "within" a certain distance of $x$, such that

$$
B_\epsilon(x):=\{y\in X | d(x,y)<\epsilon\}
$$

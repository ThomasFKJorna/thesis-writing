# Abelian group

An **Abelian group** is a [[Group]]  $G={f_1,...,f_i,...; \circ}$ whose multiplication operation is [[Commutation|Commutative]], i.e.

$\forall i,j \quad f_i \circ f_j = f_j \circ f_i$

#+title: Boundary
#+filetags: "functional analysis" topology definition

Given a [[id:e997346e-b3dd-48da-8597-93acac415fa1][Metric Space]] $(X,d)$, the *boundary* $\partial A$ of a subset $A \subseteq X$ is the set of all [[id:761df6b4-1623-47aa-a996-6e6d50ac0e8e][Boundary points]] for $A$.

#+title: Boundary
#+filetags: "functional analysis" topology definition

Given a [[file:20210416111039-metric_space.org][Metric Space]] $(X,d)$, the *boundary* $\partial A$ of a subset $A \subseteq X$ is the set of all [[file:20210416112750-boundary_points.org][Boundary points]] for $A$.

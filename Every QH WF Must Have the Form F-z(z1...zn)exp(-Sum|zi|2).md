# Every QH WF must look like this

$$
\psi(z_i...z_n)=f(z_i...z_n)e^{-\sum_{i=1}^n\abs{z_i}^2/4l_B^2}
$$

with $f(z_i...z_n)$ an analytic function of \$z<sub>i</sub>\$s
